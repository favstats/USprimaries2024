Methodology

In collaboration with Who Targets Me, we monitored 18 political advertisers in United State during the 2024 US Presidential Primaries to better understand how campaigns use different targeting methods made available by Meta. To do this, we used data from the Meta Ad Library, using the new â€˜Audienceâ€™ data which gives some detail on how pages target their ads.

To better understand the election, we kept only advertisers who:

  1. Advertised in the last 7 days (Jun 4th - Jun 10th 2023)
  2. Advertised in the last 30 days (May 12th - Jun 10th 2023)
Note

Meta only provides 7, 30 and 90 days windows for the targeting data in their Ad Library. Metaâ€™s data also lags by a few days. Report will be updated as soon as new data is available.
